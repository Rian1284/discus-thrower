Greasing the Groove Every day · Non-negotiable
Every time you walk past the bar Never to failure · 50% max
Pull-up Negative × 2–3
You cannot do a pull-up yet. So GTG is negatives — jump to top, lower in 5s, walk away. 6–8 times per day. This is how you get your first pull-up within 6–10 weeks.
Dead Hang × 20–30s
Between or instead of negatives. Builds grip and shoulder joint tolerance. Don't skip this.
Lateral Raise × 10 (rest days)
Very light. On non-training days only. Sub-threshold — never to failure. Adds cumulative delt volume without recovery cost.
Switch to full pull-ups when ready
Once you can do 1 clean pull-up, replace negatives with 1–2 full reps each pass. This compounds to 15+ reps/day without touching recovery.
